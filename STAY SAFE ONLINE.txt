Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)
Always verify links before clicking—especially emails or messages claiming to be from banks, colleges, or delivery services.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, social media, and banking apps.
Use strong, unique passwords for different platforms; avoid using your name, DOB, or simple patterns.
Do not share personal information (Aadhaar, phone number, college ID) on public platforms unnecessarily.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email.
Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

UPI / Financial Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money via UPI.
Do not scan random QR codes or accept unknown “collect requests” on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.

If Something Goes Wrong
Immediately report cyber fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance
