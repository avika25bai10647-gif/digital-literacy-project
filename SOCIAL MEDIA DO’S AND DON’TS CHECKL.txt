SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS CHECKLIST

DO’S:
Think before posting—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Maintain privacy—limit sharing of personal or sensitive information.
Use professional language, especially in academic or public discussions.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Be respectful and considerate while commenting or messaging others.

DON’TS:
Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Avoid oversharing personal details like address, phone number, or location.
Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
Avoid sharing unverified news or rumors.
Do not damage your digital reputation by posting unprofessional content.
